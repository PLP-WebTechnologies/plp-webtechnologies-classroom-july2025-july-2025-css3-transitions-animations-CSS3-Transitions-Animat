🍰 Simple Vanilla Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  2. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients and milk alternately.
  5. Pour into greased cake pan and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–35 mins.

📖 Full Recipe Steps

Follow these instructions carefully:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Mix dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt) in a bowl.
  6. Add dry mix and milk alternately into the butter mixture.
  7. Pour into greased pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  8. Cool before serving. Enjoy! 🎂
